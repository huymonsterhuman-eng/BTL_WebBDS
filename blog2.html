5 Cash-Flow Strategies for Property Investors

Pick the right asset, project rental yield, and finance safely

Published: Jan 10, 2025 • Category: Investment

Core playbook

  1. Asset fit: Choose formats that rent fast in your target district (2BR business hubs, studios near universities).
  2. Yield math: Model gross and net yield; include vacancy, management, and capex reserves.
  3. Debt safety: Keep DSCR buffer; test +1–2% interest shock before committing.
  4. Lease terms: Favor 12–24 month leases with annual escalations and quality tenants.
  5. Exit clarity: Define hold vs. flip triggers (cap rate compression, new infrastructure, or rent peak).

Quick worksheet

Target a net yield that beats your cost of capital by at least 2–3%. Benchmark comps in the same building, not just the neighborhood. If you’re eyeing furnished rentals, price in refresh cycles every 18–24 months.
