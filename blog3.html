Luxury Apartment Design Trends for 2025

Green, sustainable, and smart living elevated

Published: Jan 05, 2025 • Category: Design

Top themes

  • Biophilic living: Bigger balconies, indoor greens, and natural light corridors.
  • Sustainable materials: Low-VOC finishes, recycled stone, FSC-certified wood.
  • Smart & seamless: App-driven access, climate control, and energy dashboards.

How to evaluate units

Check glazing quality, cross-ventilation, and noise attenuation. For smart features, ensure open protocols and upgradable hubs so you aren’t locked into a single vendor. Amenity curation matters—coworking lounges, wellness spaces, and EV-ready parking add tangible value.
